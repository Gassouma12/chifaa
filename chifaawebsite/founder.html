ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Maha Jouini

AI GOVERNANCE EXPERT · FOUNDER OF CHIFAA · CANCER SURVIVOR

"A quote by Maha Here"

Maha Jouini is an AI governance expert, digital policy advocate, and communication strategist working at the intersection of technology, ethics, and social impact. She currently serves as Digital Communication Officer at the African Union Development Agency (AUDA-NEPAD) in Johannesburg and is the Vice President of the Agence Francophone et Africaine de l'Intelligence Artificielle (AFRIA).

With a strong background in public policy, advocacy, and responsible AI, Maha contributes to international initiatives shaping ethical and human-centered artificial intelligence. Her work focuses on inclusive AI governance, digital rights, and the role of technology in advancing social justice across Africa and the MENA region.

She is the author of two books: “الرقصة الأخيرة من قرطاج إلى الصين” and “عاشقة من إفريقية” — reflecting her deep interest in culture, identity, and storytelling.

Beyond her professional work, Maha is a cancer survivor. Her personal journey — a true butterfly story of transformation — inspired the creation of CHIFAA, a project dedicated to supporting women facing chronic diseases through compassionate technology, knowledge sharing, and community solidarity.
